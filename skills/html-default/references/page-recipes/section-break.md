# Recipe: Section Break

Use as a chapter divider or emphasis reset between dense pages.

## Structure Signals

- One bold section label or numbered part marker.
- One short thematic statement.
- Sparse composition with strong pacing contrast from surrounding pages.

## Good Use Cases

- PART 01 / PART 02 dividers.
- Pivot from context to evidence.
- Short emotional or narrative reset.

## Avoid

- Explanatory body copy.
- Cards, charts, or heavy bullet structures.
